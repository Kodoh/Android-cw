The Week Select screen displays all the weeks in the selected month (hence by default it will be the current month). By clicking on any of the weeks, it will then take you to the Main Page showing the sessions associated with that week. By clicking on the right arrow, it will display the weeks associated with the next month (and vice versa for the left arrow).
